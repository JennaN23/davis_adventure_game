Behold the famous UC Davis eggheads. The library is open from 7:30 to 12:00 am and here you can always find poor Ochem students with their nose in a book. If your desire to study is unusually strong, the 24-hour study rooms are the place for you.

Here you must search for knowledge.. The knowledge of the final destination. We know that the fog originates from a hall on campus.. But which? The famous bookhead will offer you some insight.

Bookhead: "Humph"
